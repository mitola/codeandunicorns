<p style="text-align: center;">
  For thy sun has long forgotten what it means to be alive,<br /> For he has scoured the skies with brothers of mothers unaccounted for.
</p>

<p style="text-align: center;">
  How long has been, since his eyes have saw,<br /> How long until he rests again in rumble of the void?
</p>

<p style="text-align: center;">
  Circling in the years one hundred a million,<br /> Around the grand keeper of souls,<br /> Around himself planets moving as electrons around neutrons in orbits unknown,<br /> Time, whatâ€™s the reason, for things to be as they are?
</p>

<p style="text-align: center;">
  Where have has he came from and where will he go?<br /> Is consciousness predefined on cosmos as whole,<br /> Or are we only neurons in a being of all?
</p>

<p style="text-align: center;">
  The answers elude me,<br /> They chase me in dreams,<br /> But never will I ever stop thinking,<br /> Why is it so?
</p>
