<p style="text-align: center;">
  Whale-ing around the cosmos on engines old and rusty,<br /> Stopping at clusters on the way,<br /> To trade, to live to breathe.
</p>

<p style="text-align: center;">
  For I am a whale, many miles long,<br /> That’s it’s ocean is space,<br /> And air an atmosphere of any kind.
</p>

<p style="text-align: center;">
  Through machinery inside my nose,<br /> Conservation and conversion.
</p>

<p style="text-align: center;">
  From cluster to cluster,<br /> At ports the biggest I trade and share.
</p>

<p style="text-align: center;">
  For the pirate ants,<br /> Of millions in number,<br /> The hunters of species of mine.
</p>

<p style="text-align: center;">
  I travel beyond the point of light.
</p>
